Peanut Butter Toast

Description

Peanut butter toast is often underestimated by those who have not been inveloped by its savory grasp. However, this dish is one that will take your breath away and make you wonder why you have been living life in the dark for so many years.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Take bread out of package and put two slices into the toaster.
  2. Toast the bread till golden brown on both sides and then put on a plate.
  3. Scoop peanut butter out of its container using a knife and spread it heavily on top of the toasted bread.
  4. Take a bite and enjoy the magic!
