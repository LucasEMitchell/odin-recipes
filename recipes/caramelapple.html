Caramel Apple

Description

Caramel Apples are a staple for fall and make your day that much sweeter. This fall treat will change your life for the better I garuntee it!

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Sweetend Condensed Milk (1 can)
  • Toppings (cookie pieces, chocolate, sprinkles, etc)

Steps

  1. Wash an apple and dry it off and set it aside.
  2. Take the sweetend condensed milk and place it in boiling water for 2 hours.
  3. Prepare the toppings.
  4. Take sweetened condensed milk out of boiling water, open the can and put contents in a seperate bowl.
  5. scewer an apple with a fork or scewer and dip in the caramel, moving it around until the entire outside is coated with caramel.
  6. Dip in toppings of choice and let sit until the caramel hardens.
  7. Enjoy!
