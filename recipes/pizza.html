Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This recipe is for a pepperoni pizza, a delicious italian bread based food. It was originally from Italy, but has now been adopted by the U.S. and created into small pieces of heaven on earth for those who are willing to take a slice!!!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Buy Pizza Dough and roll it out into a circular shape on a piece of parchment paper making certain to keep the dough at least an 1/8 inch thick when flattened.
  2. Take the tomato sauce and scoop out 1/2 a cup of sauce onto the dough and spread it over the dough with the back of a spoon leaving a half inch around the perimeter for the crust.
  3. Take a handful of mozzarella cheese and spread it out over the sauce on the dough until the sauce is barely visible underneath.
  4. Lastly, place slices of pepperoni across the top. Place as many as you wish!
  5. Put in the oven for 15-25 minutes, until the cheese and edges of the crust are golden brown.
  6. Let cool for five minutes and then dig in!
